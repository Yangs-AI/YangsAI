## Scope

This direction focuses on robust multimodal reasoning where social, cultural, and contextual priors matter.

## Current Questions

- How can models distinguish factual evidence from cultural assumptions?
- Which evaluation setup best captures context-aware failure modes?
- How should multilingual and multimodal signals be fused?

## Milestones

1. Expand culturally diverse evaluation sets.
2. Build error taxonomies for context-sensitive failures.
3. Propose training recipes and release benchmark reports.
