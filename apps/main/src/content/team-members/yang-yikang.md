## Profile

I am currently a Ph.D. student at the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences.
My research focuses on the emerging paradigm of Artificial Intelligence Designed Artificial Intelligence (AIDAI), where AI techniques are used to autonomously design AI systems.

I also work on graph generation, with a focus on graph diffusion models that generate realistic and diverse graph structures for practical applications.

## Research Interests

- Artificial Intelligence Designed Artificial Intelligence
- Graph Diffusion Models
